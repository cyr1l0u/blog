Online webinars are great, but many engineers still prefer live workshops – they’re an excellent opportunity for unrestricted 2-way communication and exchange of ideas – so I decided to turn a few of my best webinars (or webinar tracks) into workshops, and Gabi Gerber, the wonderful organizer of Data Center days in Switzerland took over the logistics, resulting in the first-ever Data Center Fabrics workshop in Zurich in late March.

As always, it’s crucial to get the fundamentals right, so the live workshop includes the topics you need to get your vendor selection or planning process started, from requirements to common architectures. You’ll also get access to the complete Data Center Fabrics webinar (over 12 hours of content), which covers details of hardware and software architectures of all major data center switching vendors including Arista, Brocade, Cisco, Dell, HP and Juniper.

All you need to do to attend this exciting event is to register – the registration form is waiting for you at the bottom of this page.
