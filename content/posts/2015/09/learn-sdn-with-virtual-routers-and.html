Notes:

  • Open-source fork of Vyatta is still available.
  • Cisco IOSv is available within OnePK and might be exportable from it (not sure about the legality of doing that);
  • Numerous images are exportable from Cisco VIRL(however, see above);
  • Cisco ACI labs are available on dcloud.cisco.com (not sure whether it’s available to non-customers);
  • Firewall and load balancing vendors offering virtual versions of their products include A10, Brocade, Cisco ASA, Citrix Netscaler and VPX Express, Checkpoint, F5 BIG-IP VE, Fortinet, and Palo Alto.
