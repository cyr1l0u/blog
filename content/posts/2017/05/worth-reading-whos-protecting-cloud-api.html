Everyone loves talking about cloud security (or lack thereof) and focuses on protecting workloads, data in the cloud… but have you ever asked the question “how protected is the cloud management API?”

Jan Bervar did exactly that in one of his blog posts and while he still hasn’t published the follow-up articles he promised (more than a year ago), at the very least you should start thinking about this challenge.

Want to know more? Matthias Luft covered this topic in more details in the Building Next-Generation Data Center online course.
