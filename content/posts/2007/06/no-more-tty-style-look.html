--- url: /2007/06/no-more-tty-style-look.html title: "No more TTY-style look" date: "2007-06-02T10:18:00.000+02:00" --- I've been considering adding the Digg-it button to my blog for a while, but never found time to do it properly ... until a rainy quiet Saturday morning when I've stumbled across a great how-to article. Excellent tip, but the digg-it text was displayed on white background which clashed horrendously with the dark-blue surroundings. So I finally gave up on using white text on dark background (which I sort-of fancy as I've started on old Digital VT100 terminals), hopefully also making the blog easier to read.
