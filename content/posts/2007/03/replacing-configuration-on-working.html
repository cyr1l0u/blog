--- title: "Replacing configuration on a working router" date: "2007-03-03T10:28:00.000+01:00" tags: [ network management,configuration,EEM ] --- In my IP Corner article Replacing Configuration on a Working Router, I'm describing how you can use the Configuration Replace and Configuration Rollback features of Cisco IOS to replace configuration you've managed to break with a working one.
