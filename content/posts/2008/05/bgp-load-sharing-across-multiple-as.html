--- title: "BGP load sharing across multiple AS-paths" date: "2008-05-14T07:05:00.002+02:00" tags: [ BGP ] --- Sebastian Majewski has contributed a short article to the CT3 Wiki explaining a hidden Cisco IOS router configuration command that can bypass the limitations of the IBGP multipath load sharing in scenarios where the paths across which you want to load-share cross different upstream autonomous systems.
