--- url: /2008/02/running-ospf-across-frame-relay.html title: "Running OSPF across Frame Relay" date: "2008-02-24T18:43:00.002+01:00" tags: [ OSPF,WAN,links ] --- The various models you can use to run OSPF over partially-meshed WAN (usually Frame Relay) are complex enough to make you scream ... or switch to EIGRP :) Arden Packeer did an excellent job in his three-part tutorial describing broadcast and non-broadcast network types, point-to-multipoint network type and point-to-point links over Frame Relay.

If you still have problems with OSPF after reading all three tutorials, use point-to-point subinterfaces.
